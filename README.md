# CulinariPolicy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

CookbookBuilder (“the App”) is developed and maintained as an independent software project. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data.

⸻

1. Overview

We respect your privacy.

We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal data.

The App is designed to function locally on your device whenever possible. Your recipes, ingredients, notes, and usage remain under your control.

⸻

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

* Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, address)
* User accounts or login data
* Location data
* Recipe content or user-generated content
* Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
* Device identifiers
* Crash reports tied to user identity
* Advertising identifiers

We have no interest in your data and do not operate any tracking or analytics systems.

⸻

3. Local Data Storage

All recipe data is stored locally on your device using Apple’s native storage systems (such as SwiftData / CoreData depending on implementation).

This data:

* Never leaves your device unless explicitly exported by you
* Is not accessible to the developer
* Is not transmitted to any external servers controlled by us

⸻

4. Claude API Integration (Optional Feature)

The App may include optional AI-assisted features powered by the Claude API (Anthropic).

If you choose to use these features:

* Requests are sent directly from your device to Anthropic’s API
* You are responsible for your own API key
* The developer does not receive, store, or proxy your API key
* The developer does not log, inspect, or retain any prompts or AI responses

Important:

* Your API key is stored securely on your device (e.g. Keychain)
* You may remove or disable it at any time
* Any data sent to Claude is governed by Anthropic’s Privacy Policy

We do not have access to what you ask the AI or what it returns.

⸻

5. No Cloud Sync or Accounts

The App does not require:

* Accounts
* Sign-in
* Cloud storage
* Remote databases

Your data remains local unless you choose to export or share it.

⸻

6. Third-Party Services

The only potential third-party service used is:

* Anthropic Claude API (only when you explicitly enable AI features)

We do not integrate analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

⸻

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store user data:

* There is no central database to breach
* No user profiles exist on our servers
* Security is primarily dependent on your device security

We still recommend enabling device-level protections such as Face ID / Touch ID / passcodes.

⸻

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, no child-specific data handling is required.

⸻

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, changes will be posted within the App or repository. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any updates.

⸻

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the project’s GitHub repository or listed support channel.

⸻

In Plain English:

We don’t track you.
We don’t store your recipes.
We don't collect anything.
If you use Claude, it goes straight from your device to Anthropic using your own API key.
